how it started.

               

Bird-e-portfolio came to be through a clever play on words incorporating my nickname, Sarah Bird/Birdy. I’ve spent my lifetime following my passion for creativity and making art. From decoupage to digital advertising, I find a way to visually communicate with the world.

           
           
               

where it's going.

               

I am fortunate to be pursuing my AAS in Digital Media at Northwest Vista College in San Antonio, Tx. My objective is to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge to become a successful and pertinent professional in the industry.
